About Me

Hi I'm Sakha!

You’ve just come to the right place to know about me!

I’m a self-taught photographer & an amateur designer from the Southernmost Atoll in the Maldives, based in the capital concrete jungle: Malè.

My inspiration in photography has always come from the people I’ve met and places I’ve travelled in my childhood.

The first time I photographed somebody was using a camcorder, at the age of 6 forcing my sister to model for me. Looking back, I have come a long way and I’m still working on polishing my skills.

Over the years, I’ve worked with businesses and people of all types & backgrounds.

My experience covers portraiture, product, landscape, wedding, events and architectural photography.

I consider myself incredibly lucky, to be doing what I love to do, and share my passion for photography with anyone and everyone who approach & work with me. If you would like to consider working together, I would love to speak with you.
